## About the Company

Swans helps law firms embed AI capabilities into their daily operations. Focus is on implementation over research, deploying AI into real-world organizational systems.

## Key Responsibilities

- Deploy practical AI solutions including custom integrations, automation workflows, and LLM-powered tools
- Build connectors, agents, and workflows for client systems
- Run enablement sessions that drive adoption among non-technical teams
- Work simultaneously on client engagements and internal projects
- Stay current with Claude and Google AI ecosystems

## Required Qualifications

- Applied AI expertise, builder mentality
- Technical breadth across APIs and automation platforms
- Autonomy in project execution
- Enablement mindset
- Curiosity about emerging capabilities
- Clear communication with non-technical stakeholders
- B2-level professional English fluency

## Ideal Background

- 2-5 years deploying AI tools professionally
- Experience with LLM ecosystems (Claude/Gemini), automation platforms (Make, n8n, Zapier), REST APIs, MCP frameworks, custom AI agents, and team adoption strategies
- Portfolio examples preferred

## Not a Fit If

- Seeking pure research roles
- Lacking deployed systems experience
- Wanting "chill" environments
- Preferring input-based assessment
- Needing structured corporate settings

## Compensation

Competitive salary, performance-based bonus, flexible benefits, and substantial professional development investment.

## Location Notes

Fully remote with quarterly in-person meetups (USA, Tokyo, Dubai, Bangkok). Currently not considering candidates residing in the US.
