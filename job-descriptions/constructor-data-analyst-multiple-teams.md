## About the Company

Constructor is the next-generation platform for search and discovery in ecommerce, built to explicitly optimize for metrics like revenue, conversion rate, and profit. The search engine is entirely built in-house utilizing transformers and generative LLMs. Powers over 1 billion queries every day across 150 languages and roughly 100 countries. Used by companies like Sephora, Under Armour, and Petco. U.S. based, founded in 2019.

## About the Role

Hiring Data Analysts across multiple teams: Data Science Integrations, Prospect Data Science Integrations, Ranking, Recommendations, and AI Shopping Agent. Work at the intersection of data, product, and machine learning.

## Key Responsibilities

- Analyze user behavior and large-scale datasets to identify patterns, issues, and opportunities for improvement
- Design, run, and analyze A/B tests to evaluate product changes and measure impact on key business metrics
- Define and validate metrics that reflect both user experience and business outcomes
- Build dashboards, reports, and data tools to support decision-making across teams
- Partner with customers, engineering, product, and business teams to translate insights into product improvements
- Work with SQL, Python, and distributed data systems to process and analyze large volumes of data

## Core Skills

- Strong analytical skills and experience solving business problems using data
- Solid knowledge of SQL and experience working with large datasets
- Experience with Python for data analysis (pandas, NumPy, visualization libraries)
- Understanding of A/B testing and statistical concepts

## Product & Data Understanding

- Understanding of machine learning pipelines, training data quality, and model evaluation
- Familiarity with ranking, recommendations, or search relevance is a plus
- Experience defining metrics, building metric trees, and analyzing product funnels

## Communication & Ownership

- Ability to communicate insights clearly and translate business needs into analytical solutions
- Comfortable working with multiple stakeholders in a fast-paced environment

- Experience working in e-commerce or product analytics
