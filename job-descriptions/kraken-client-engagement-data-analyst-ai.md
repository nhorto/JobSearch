## About the Company

Kraken is a cryptocurrency exchange focused on accelerating global crypto adoption. Fully remote company operating across 70+ countries.

## Key Responsibilities

### AI Orchestration & Outcome Delivery
- Design AI agent workflows addressing operational problems beyond basic tools
- Identify high-impact AI opportunities within Client Engagement
- Establish data foundations and tagging logic for accurate AI decisions
- Test and refine new AI capabilities before broad rollout

### Data Structure & AI Readiness
- Design data models supporting AI tagging and classification
- Audit Zendesk platforms ensuring AI consumption compatibility
- Map support data categories to desired AI outputs
- Identify gaps between current and needed data structures

### Reporting & Insights
- Build dashboards surfacing AI performance and operational metrics
- Develop "Echo Report" and similar AI-driven outputs
- Monitor feedback loops between AI suggestions and human behavior
- Identify emerging support data patterns

### Cross-Functional Translation
- Partner with AI and engineering leaders
- Translate operational needs into technical requirements
- Build AI literacy across the Client Engagement team
- Communicate data changes to non-technical stakeholders

## Required Qualifications

- 3-5 years data analyst or operations analyst experience
- Experience coordinating AI agents (OpenClaw, LangChain, CrewAI)
- Strong SQL skills
- Zendesk or comparable CX platform expertise
- BI tool proficiency (Looker, Mixpanel, etc.)
- Data taxonomy and tagging framework experience
- Ability translating between operational and technical language
- Understanding of model-ready data structures
- Teaching and mentorship capability

## Nice-to-Have Skills

- AI tagging pipelines and LLM-based classification experience
- Python or scripting expertise
- Crypto/digital assets background
- Process mining and workflow automation tools familiarity
- Lean Six Sigma certification

## Location

Remote — available in Brazil, Poland, Romania, Czech Republic, Cyprus, Slovenia, Malta, Latvia, Slovakia, Estonia, Chile, Uruguay, El Salvador, Panama, Costa Rica, Paraguay, Argentina, and Mexico.
