## About the Company

Nivoda is a rapidly expanding global marketplace for the jewelry industry with teams in London, New York, Hong Kong, and Mumbai. Prioritizes three core pillars: quality, speed, and price.

## Role Overview

Dedicated data science position centered on creating predictive models and forecasting systems to enhance supply chain performance. Not a general analytics role.

## Key Responsibilities

- Develop delivery prediction models that provide precise dates instead of estimated ranges
- Incorporate hub performance metrics and routing logic to improve accuracy
- Build demand forecasting models for Express offerings across diamonds, gemstones, and jewelry
- Determine optimal inventory levels by location and product category
- Identify opportunities to reduce lead times and enhance fulfillment accuracy
- Collaborate with data engineering teams to establish data infrastructure requirements

## Required Qualifications

- 5-7+ years as a data scientist with proven deployment of production models
- Expertise in machine learning, statistical modeling, and forecasting
- Ability to work independently with minimal supervision
- Strong collaboration and communication skills
- Comfort navigating ambiguous, fast-paced environments

## Preferred Qualifications

- Background in supply chain, logistics, or fulfillment operations
- Experience at fast-commerce or on-demand logistics companies
- Familiarity with real-time data systems at scale
- Proximity to Netherlands or London hubs

## Location

Remote — European timezone. No visa sponsorship available.

## Benefits

Flexible hours, remote work, unlimited holiday allowance, and professional development opportunities.
