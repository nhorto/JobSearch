## About the Company

ElevenLabs is an AI research and product company that develops human-like AI voice models. Serves millions of users and thousands of businesses including Deutsche Telekom and Meta. Raised $781M at $11B valuation. Three main platforms: ElevenAgents (voice/chat agents at scale), ElevenCreative (speech, music, image, video across 70+ languages), ElevenAPI (AI audio foundational models).

## Role Overview

Design and build tooling for GTM, operations, and finance teams. Embed within specific teams, partnering with sales, marketing, operations, and product leaders to identify pain points and deliver solutions rapidly.

## Key Responsibilities

- Design and implement internal tools and infrastructure supporting revenue teams
- Build AI agents to automate processes and integrate data across multiple systems
- Partner with strategy teams to understand and optimize customer and sales lifecycles
- Launch experiments to identify operational scaling opportunities
- Deliver technical enablement through education, tooling, and streamlined workflows
- Rapidly prototype full-stack features with immediate business impact

## Required Qualifications

- Strong full-stack engineering skills (Python and SQL minimum)
- Builder mentality with ability to work independently without perfect specifications
- Deep product intuition with focus on business impact
- Passion for AI applications in B2B sales, marketing, operations, and finance
- Experience with data, AI agents, LLMs, prompting, and integration patterns

## Location

Remote (global). Offices in Bangalore, Dublin, London, New York, San Francisco, Tokyo, and Warsaw.

## Benefits

Annual stipends for professional development, social travel, co-working, plus annual company offsites in international locations.
