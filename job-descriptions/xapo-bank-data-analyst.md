# Data Analyst — Xapo Bank

**Source:** https://job-boards.greenhouse.io/xapo61/jobs/7572078003
**Date Saved:** 2026-04-01

## About the Company

Xapo Bank is headquartered in Gibraltar. The role supports Finance and Treasury teams with data analytics and reporting.

## Key Responsibilities

- Transform transactional and financial data into strategic insights driving company expansion
- Present findings clearly through visualizations and compelling narratives
- Build and oversee complete data pipeline systems
- Create and maintain Looker dashboards enabling stakeholder self-service analytics
- Partner strategically with Finance, Treasury, and cross-functional teams, translating business needs into technical requirements
- Guide stakeholders in daily data application and offer insights-based recommendations
- Identify financial efficiency opportunities and deliver strategic business guidance
- Maintain data integrity and establish unified reporting sources
- Deploy automation tools and predictive modeling capabilities
- Continuously seek improvements in data processes

## Required Qualifications

- 2+ years of experience in a dedicated data analytics role
- Strong SQL proficiency (BigQuery experience valuable)
- Robust data modeling competency
- Python analytics experience
- Excellent English communication with ability to explain complex insights to non-technical audiences
- Organizational strength managing concurrent projects with meticulous attention
- Exceptional analytical and critical thinking capabilities

## Preferred Qualifications

- Looker developer expertise
- Finance/fintech/cryptocurrency background
- GCP familiarity including BigQuery, Cloud Functions, and Vertex AI

## Location

Remote — Work from Anywhere.

## Other Requirements

Dedicated workspace, reliable high-speed internet, alignment with company values.
