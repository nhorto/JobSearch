# Middle Data Analyst — Growe

**Source:** https://growe.com/career/vacancy/4638861101
**Date Saved:** 2026-04-01

## About the Company

Growe is a company with a BI & Analytics department focused on partner traffic, user behavior, and campaign metrics.

## Core Responsibilities

- Analyze partner traffic, user behavior, and campaign metrics
- Build automated reports using Tableau, SQL, and Google Sheets
- Validate and prepare data from multiple sources
- Support affiliate and brand teams with performance insights
- Monitor data quality
- Conduct hypothesis testing
- Present findings clearly to stakeholders

## Required Qualifications

- 3+ years in data analytics
- Proficiency in SQL, Tableau, Excel/Google Sheets
- Understanding of traffic sources and large databases
- Strong data validation and automation skills
- Cross-functional team experience
- Clear presentation abilities
- Upper-intermediate English

## Desired Traits

- Analytical thinking, proactivity
- Ability to simplify complex concepts
- Adaptability, multitasking capability, and attention to detail
- Alignment with company values emphasizing teamwork, results-driven goals, and embracing change
