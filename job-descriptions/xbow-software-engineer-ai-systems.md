## About the Company

XBOW builds autonomous security platforms using AI to discover and exploit vulnerabilities faster than traditional methods. Founded by GitHub Copilot creator Oege de Moor and backed by major investors, the company has identified thousands of zero-days and ranked #1 on HackerOne's leaderboard.

## Core Responsibilities

- Design prompt flows and orchestrations for LLM-powered systems with high accuracy
- Build production-grade, maintainable AI software stacks
- Create evaluation frameworks and conduct performance testing at scale
- Collaborate across AI, engineering, and security teams on end-to-end solutions
- Own projects from conception through deployment and monitoring
- Research emerging LLM advancements to enhance system capabilities

## Required Qualifications

- Demonstrated expertise building software with LLMs, including prompting and agentic orchestration
- Strong production software engineering skills
- TypeScript experience or quick language-learning ability
- Independent problem-solving in ambiguous situations
- Comfort with fast-paced startup research environments
- MSc or equivalent in computer science, math, physics, or machine learning

## Bonus Qualifications

Security sector experience, open-source contributions, PhD-level background, or startup familiarity.

## Compensation & Benefits

$100K–$350K salary plus equity package; remote flexibility with periodic in-person collaboration.
