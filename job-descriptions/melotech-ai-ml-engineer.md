- 3+ years of hands-on ML engineering experience building production systems at major tech companies or high-growth startups

- Extensive MLOps experience (Docker, Kubernetes, model versioning, monitoring)
- Proven track record deploying ML models in high-availability production environments
- Self-directed with ability to architect complex systems independently
- Passion for music, video, or social media
